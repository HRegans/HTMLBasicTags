Reasons to LOVE corgis

Three Reasons:

  1. Ears: corgi's are known for their significantly large ears for their little bodies; a feature you can't help to love.
  2. Smile: everyone likes to see a dog smile, but corgi's have such a distinctly adorable smile that's impossible to forget.
  3. Legs: this is usually a corgi owner's favorite feature about their corgi. Corgi's grew their fame from their short stature, yet lengthy bodies.
